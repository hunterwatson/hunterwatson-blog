Preheat oven to **375ºF**

1/2 Cup **Margarine**

/*Beat*/

1 Cup **Sugar**

/*Beat*/

3 Tablespoon **Canola Oil**  
3 Tablespoon **Water**  
2 Teaspoon **Baking Powder**

/*Mix*/

1/4 Teaspoon **Salt**  
2 Teaspoon **Baking Powder**   
1/4 Cup **Cocoa**  
1 **Mashed Banana**  
1 Teaspoon **Vanilla**  

/*Mix*/

Fold 1 Cup **Flour**   
Fold 1/4 Cup **Water**  
Fold 1 Cup **Flour**  
Fold 1/4 Cup **Water**  

Fill muffin tins 3/4 full
Bake at **375ºF** for **25 minutes**

Let cool for **15 minutes**
